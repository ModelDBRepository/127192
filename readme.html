Matlab code for generating hierarchical networks, simulating activity
spreading, and calculating the proportion of simulation runs with
limited sustained activity (LSA). These routines were used for the
article

Marcus Kaiser and Claus Hilgetag (2010).
Optimal hierarchical modular topologies for producing limited
sustained activation of neural networks.
Frontiers in Neuroinformatics.
May 2010 | Volume 4 | Article 8
doi: 10.3389/fninf.2010.00008

The article and code are available at
http://www.biological-networks.org/pubs/Kaiser2010Frontiers.pdf
and
http://www.biological-networks.org/pubs/suppl/Kaiser2010Frontiers.zip
